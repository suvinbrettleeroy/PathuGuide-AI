Understanding Your Role

What This Role Does
  • Collect, clean, and analyze data to uncover insights
  • Create visualizations and reports for stakeholders
  • Work with databases and statistical tools
  • Help companies make data-driven decisions
How It Works in Real Companies

Data Analysts work closely with business teams, marketing, and product managers. You'll spend your time querying databases, creating dashboards in tools like Tableau or Power BI, and presenting findings to help guide business strategy. Most companies offer remote or hybrid work options.

Why This Suits You

Based on your department background and preference for remote work, data analysis is an excellent fit. With your available 2-4 hours/day, you can master the necessary tools within your 6-month roadmap.
